Company Overview

LYNEXOS is a dynamic and integrated oil services provider specializing in logistics, operations, and expert workforce solutions for the oil and gas industry. We supply high-caliber professionals for both onshore and offshore projects, ensuring operational efficiency, safety, and success across energy infrastructures.

With a name that reflects our mission—Leading Your Needs in Oil EXploration & Services—LYNEXOS brings decades of business acumen and hands-on field experience to every project. Our strength lies in bridging critical manpower needs with energy demands while delivering tailored support to the oil & gas sector.
