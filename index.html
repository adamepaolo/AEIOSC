Company Overview

LYNEXOS is a dynamic and integrated oil services provider specializing in logistics, operations, and expert workforce solutions for the oil and gas industry. Our expertise and current experiences have the ability to supply high-caliber professionals for both onshore and offshore projects, ensuring operational efficiency, safety, and success across energy infrastructures.

With a name that reflects our mission—Leading Your Needs in Oil EXploration & Services—LYNEXOS brings decades of business acumen and hands-on field experience to every project. Our strength lies in bridging critical manpower needs with energy demands while delivering tailored support to the oil & gas sector.

We are proud to partner with major Libyan oil companies, including:

  • Waha Oil Company
  • Sirte Oil Company
  • Akakus Oil Operations
  • Zueitina Oil Company
  • Harouge Oil Operation
  • Mellitah Oil and Gas
  • Mabruk Oil Operations

These collaborations underscore our commitment to excellence, compliance, and talent-driven success.

Business Profile

An accomplished entrepreneur with over 20 years of cross-sector experience spanning printing, event management, manufacturing, and general contracting. The founder has successfully established and managed multiple ventures, demonstrating a consistent ability to deliver high-impact solutions.

With a strategic blend of administrative insight, technical expertise, and business foresight, LYNEXOS is positioned to serve the oil & gas industry with professionalism and reliability. In response to increasing manpower demands, the business is expanding to better meet industry needs.

Career Timeline

  • 2005 – The Beginning: Founded Dar Taha Printing House, offering premium printing services with a strong market reputation.
  • 2011 – Entry into General Contracting: Established a contracting company executing projects like equipping restaurants, hotels, camps, and field sites.
  • 2015 – Strategic Expansion: Transformed into Taha Printing Company and launched Libya Company for Exhibitions and Conferences.
  • Manufacturing and Supply Development: Founded Al-Jawda Printing Factory producing pizza and sweets boxes for local businesses.
